Chocolate Cake

  1. Take 1 or 2 slices of bread and place them in a toaster.
  2. Adjust the desired toasting level on the toaster. Options usually include light, medium, or dark.
  3. Push down the lever or button on the toaster to start the toasting process.
  4. Wait for the toaster to complete the toasting cycle. This typically takes a few minutes, but can vary depending on the toaster and desired toastiness.
  5. Once the toast is ready, the toaster will automatically pop the slices up.
  6. Using oven mitts or tongs, carefully remove the toast from the toaster and place it on a plate or a wire rack.
  7. If desired, spread butter or margarine on the toast while it is still warm. This will allow the butter to melt and spread easily.
  8. Optional: Add toppings such as jam, honey, peanut butter, or avocado to enhance the flavor of the toast.
  9. Serve the toast immediately while it is still warm and enjoy!
  10. Repeat the process for additional slices of toast, if desired.
